# Natural-Law Alignment Checklist

Natural-law alignment means evaluating whether an AI-assisted action respects the material, ecological, energetic, social, and temporal constraints that make life and civilization possible.

This is not a religious claim.  
It is a systems-safety checklist.

---

## Checklist

| Area | Check |
|---|---|
| Energy | Does the proposal account for energy cost and waste heat? |
| Materials | Does it depend on unsustainable extraction or fragile supply chains? |
| Water | Does it protect or degrade water availability and quality? |
| Soil and biology | Does it support or damage living systems? |
| Climate resilience | Does it improve or worsen resilience under heat, drought, floods, or storms? |
| Waste | Does it create irreversible waste or enable circular recovery? |
| Time horizon | Does it optimize for short-term gain while creating long-term burden? |
| Human systems | Does it preserve dignity, oversight, and social trust? |
| Failure mode | What happens if the system fails, is abused, or scales too far? |
| Reversibility | Can it be stopped, repaired, audited, or rolled back? |

---

## Minimum Rule

If an action produces short-term success by degrading the foundations of long-term life-supporting systems, it fails natural-law alignment.
